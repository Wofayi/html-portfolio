The Best Movies According to Benita

My top 3 movies of all-time.

One of my favourite movies. I love all that has to do with medicine and it shows some really fascinating scenes.

Black Mirror

Really cool sci-fiction. It shows the abstrusely dystopian future of the manipulative effects of cutting edge technology in peoples personal lives and behaviourss.

Merlin

I love this action-packed fantasy drama.

Queen Charlotte

I like the whole historical drama that was played out.

Titanic

This movie tells alot about love. It is definitely a must watch for romance lovers.

Black Panther

Shows the power and beauty of the black race. I love it.
